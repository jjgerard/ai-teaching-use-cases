Association explorer

Pick two tag dimensions to cross-tabulate. Each heatmap row is normalized to 100% — darker cells mean a larger share of that row's case studies carry that column's tag. The ranked list surfaces the strongest pairings across all dimensions, not just the two selected, weighted by how much they beat the catalog-wide baseline (lift), with a minimum of 5 case studies behind each claim.

Hover a cell, or click a ranked pairing, to see matching case studies here.

Predictor lab

A multinomial logistic regression, trained in your browser on the — catalogued entries. Choose what to predict and which tags to predict it from, then set a scenario below to see the model's probability distribution. With this little data the model is suggestive, not validated — read it as a second, model-based way of asking "what predicts what," alongside the lift ranking above, not as a fitted result you'd publish.

Notes on this view

  • Data: data/community.json in the case-study-catalog repo, — approved entries as of the date below. Discipline and tool are multi-select tags, so row/column percentages can sum to more than 100%.
  • Categories beyond the top 7 per dimension are folded into "+N more" to keep charts legible — click through to the association explorer's example drawer to see what's inside a folded group.
  • The predictor lab's holdout accuracy is reported alongside chance level for a reason: with — rows and multi-label tags, treat every model output here as a rough signal, not a validated result.
